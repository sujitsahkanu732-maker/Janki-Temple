Welcome to Janki Temple

Janki Temple, located in the heart of Janakpur, Nepal, is one of the most revered pilgrimage sites dedicated to Goddess Sita, also known as Janki. Rich in history and spiritual significance, it welcomes thousands of devotees from across the world.

Our Mission

We aim to preserve and promote the heritage of the temple, foster devotion, and provide a peaceful environment for worship and cultural exchange.
